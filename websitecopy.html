Fashion Design

An average person owns seven pairs of blue jeans, in the United States.

Fashion design is applying design to clothing and accessories. It is usually heavily influenced by culture attitudes, and varies over time and place. Designers get their ideas from paintings, films, or an abstarct idea.

Photography

Photography means drawing with light, in Greek.

Photography is the art of creating images by recording light or electromagnetic radiation. It is employed in many different fields of science, as well as its more direct uses for film and video production.

Interior Design

Elsie de Wolfe (1859-1950) is considered to have invented interior designing as a profession.

Interior design is the art of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve an aesthecial environment. Designers undertake the basic layout of spacewithin a building, as well as technical issues. Although designers layout space, they may not alter walls without the approval of a structural engineer.
